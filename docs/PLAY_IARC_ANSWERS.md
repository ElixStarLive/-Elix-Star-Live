## Expected rating

Likely **Teen** or regional equivalent (often **PEGI 12+** / **ESRB Teen**) because of:

- User-generated content
- Unrestricted social interaction
- In-app purchases

Google generates the exact badge after you submit.

---

## After rating

- Confirm rating certificate is **active** before production release
- If you add major features (dating, real-money gambling, etc.), **retake** the questionnaire
