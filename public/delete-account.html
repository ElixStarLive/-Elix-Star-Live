Delete Your Elix Star Live Account

Elix Star Live users can permanently delete their account and associated personal data.

Delete inside the app

  1. Sign in to Elix Star Live.
  2. Open Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the permanent deletion.

Request deletion without app access

Email us from the address registered to your account. Include your username and state that you want your Elix Star Live account deleted.

Request account deletion

What is deleted

Your account, profile, sessions, social connections, messages, content associations, device tokens, and wallet data are removed from active systems. Deletion is permanent.

Data that may be retained

Records that must be retained for fraud prevention, tax, payment, dispute, or other legal obligations may be kept only for the required retention period, as described in our Privacy Policy.

Requests are verified and processed within 30 days. Contact: info@elixstarlive.co.uk.
