Supplier Agreement

Last updated: July 15, 2026

1. Parties

This Supplier Agreement ("Agreement") is between Elix Star Live Ltd ("Buyer"), registered in England and Wales, and the supplier named in the commercial schedule or order ("Supplier").

The Shop in Elix Star Live is buy-only for end users. End users do not sell to each other. You supply goods to Elix Star Live Ltd for us to offer and sell to customers.

2. Supply of Goods

  • Supply goods as described in purchase orders or written schedules.
  • Goods must match description, quality, packaging, and labelling.
  • You must have clear title and the right to sell to us.

3. Compliance & Product Safety

  • Comply with applicable UK and destination-market product laws.
  • No illegal, counterfeit, stolen, or prohibited items.
  • Cooperate with recalls and regulator requests.

4. Intellectual Property

  • You warrant goods do not infringe third-party IP.
  • You grant us a licence to use product images/names to market and sell the goods in our Shop.

5. Pricing, Invoices & Payment

  • Prices as agreed in writing.
  • Payment terms and currency as stated in the commercial schedule (often GBP).
  • You are responsible for your own taxes.

6. Title, Risk & Returns

  • Title/risk as agreed in writing.
  • We may reject non-conforming goods.
  • Consumer returns may require credit or return stock as agreed.

7–10. Confidentiality, Liability, Term, Law

Confidential information must be protected. Supplier indemnifies Elix Star Live Ltd for defective goods, IP claims, and regulatory non-compliance (subject to non-excludable liability). Either party may terminate for material breach. Governed by England and Wales law.

Support: support@elixstarlive.co.uk

This is a standard supplier framework. A signed purchase order or commercial schedule is required for a binding supply relationship. Have a solicitor review before high-value deals.
